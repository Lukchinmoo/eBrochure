Important Notice

  • Rates are based on the supplied promo-rate price. Valid until 31 October 2026 and only for direct booking.
  • Pay by credit card with no bank charge.
  • Free cancellation 24 hours before tour departure.
  • Terms and conditions apply.
  • Participant details are required on booking.

Cancellation Policy

  • Customers will receive a full refund or credit with 24 hours notice of cancellation.
  • Customers will also receive a full refund or credit in case of operator cancellation due to weather or other unforeseen circumstances.
  • Contact us by phone to cancel or inquire about a cancellation.
  • No-shows will be charged the full price.

Recommended for Indian guests

  • For first-time Phuket visitors: Phi Phi or Phang Nga Bay.
  • For families with children: Khai Islands, Andamanda Water Park, Dolphin Show, or evening shows.
  • For premium nature lovers: Similan or Surin Islands during the open season.
  • For guests avoiding long sea days: City Tour, Spa, Elephant Care, ATV, Zipline, or stage shows.

Before booking

  • Share guest names, ages, hotel, travel date, and preferred tour.
  • Ask us to check vegetarian, Jain, Indian meal, or private transfer requests.
  • National park fees and some add-ons may be excluded depending on the tour.
  • Weather and sea conditions may affect island trips.
