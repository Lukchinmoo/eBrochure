Recommended for Indian guests

  • For first-time Phuket visitors: Phi Phi or Phang Nga Bay.
  • For families with children: Khai Islands, Andamanda Water Park, Dolphin Show, or evening shows.
  • For premium nature lovers: Similan or Surin Islands during the open season.
  • For guests avoiding long sea days: City Tour, Spa, Elephant Care, ATV, Zipline, or stage shows.

Before booking

  • Share guest names, ages, hotel, travel date, and preferred tour.
  • Ask us to check vegetarian, Jain, Indian meal, or private transfer requests.
  • National park fees and some add-ons may be excluded depending on the tour.
  • Weather and sea conditions may affect island trips.
